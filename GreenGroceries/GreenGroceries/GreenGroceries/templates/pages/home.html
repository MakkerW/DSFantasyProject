How Does FPL Scoring Work?

Fantasy Premier League players are scored as follows:

Goalkeepers & Defenders:

  • -1 point for every two goals conceded
  • +4 points for a clean sheet
  • +6 points for scoring a goal
  • +5 points for saving a penalty (goalies)
  • +1 point for making three or more saves (goalies)

Midfielders:

  • +1 point for a clean sheet
  • +5 points for sco ring

Forwards:

  • +4 points for scoring

All Positions:

  • +3 points for assisting
  • +1 point for playing up to 60 minutes
  • +2 points for playing 60 minutes or more
  • +3 points for Best Player in the Match
  • +2 points for 2nd Best Player in the Match
  • +1 point for 3rd Best Player in the Match
